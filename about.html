About CPE Sports

The official digital platform for sports in the Computer Engineering Department, with a strong focus on football and a growing coverage of other sports.

What This Platform Is

CPE Sports is designed to bring all sporting activities in the department into one place. From match fixtures and results to breaking news and updates, this platform ensures that every student stays connected to what’s happening.

Our Vision

Our goal is to build a complete sports hub for the department — starting with football, which makes up over 80% of activities, while also expanding into other sports over time.

The Department

This platform represents the Computer Engineering Department and its sporting culture. It is built to improve communication, engagement, and participation among students.
